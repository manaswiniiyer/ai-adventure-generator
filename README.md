# WanderQuest AI

**Every Journey Becomes an Adventure.**

WanderQuest AI is an AI-powered travel RPG that transforms real-world exploration into interactive adventures. Instead of providing static itineraries, the application generates personalized stories, quests, NPCs, puzzles, achievements, and multiple endings based on the user's destination, interests, and travel preferences.

---

## Inspiration

Traditional travel apps answer one question:

> **"Where should I go?"**

WanderQuest AI answers a different one:

> **"What adventure will I experience today?"**

Inspired by games like Assassin's Creed, Pokémon GO, Zelda, and tabletop RPGs, we built an AI-powered Game Master that turns every city into a playable world.

# How GPT-5.6 Was Used

GPT-5.6 is the intelligence behind WanderQuest AI.

Instead of returning a simple itinerary, GPT-5.6 dynamically creates complete RPG adventures tailored to each traveler.

The model is responsible for generating:

### Story Generation

Creates unique narratives based on:

- Destination
- Theme
- Budget
- Trip duration
- User interests

Example:

> "A forgotten royal artifact lies hidden beneath the ancient city walls. Recover it before sunset by solving clues scattered across historic landmarks."

---

### Quest Generation

Generates multiple mission types including:

- Landmark exploration
- Historical trivia
- Photography challenges
- Treasure hunts
- Observation puzzles
- Navigation missions
- Time-based quests

Every adventure contains unique objectives.

---

### NPC Dialogue

Every important landmark includes an AI-generated character.

Examples:

- Historian
- Merchant
- Explorer
- Royal Guard
- Scientist

GPT-5.6 creates natural conversations, clues, hints, and side quests for each NPC.

---

### Hint System

If a player becomes stuck, GPT-5.6 dynamically generates hints at different difficulty levels.

- Small Hint
- Medium Hint
- Complete Solution

---

### Personalized Endings

After completing the adventure, GPT-5.6 generates an ending based on the player's actions.

Possible endings include:

- Hero Ending
- Secret Ending
- Legend Ending
- Dark Ending
- Funny Ending

Every ending is unique.
